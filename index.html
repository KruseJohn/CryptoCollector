Crypto Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four cryptocurrency coins listed below -

BITCOIN, ETHEREUM, RIPPLE, and STELLAR...

By clicking on a coin you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each coin is hidden until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the coins will reset to a new value.
